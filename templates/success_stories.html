More Than a Milestone — A Movement Begins

When Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur named Beyond Career one of the most impactful startups, it meant a lot. Because this isn’t just a startup — it’s a response to a broken system where opportunities are hidden, support is limited, and real talent is often overlooked.

We built Beyond Career for students who never got a callback. For those with potential but no access. For the ones who believed in themselves, but just needed someone else to believe in them too.

This win isn’t ours alone. It belongs to everyone who showed up, trusted the process, and said, “I’m in — let’s build something better.”

And to those still figuring it out — we’re just getting started.

None of this would have been possible without Nitish Kumar — the quiet strength behind the scenes who helped build it all.
